# Chocolate Bar Hot Chocolate

This is a simple, delicious hot chocolate made directly from a milk chocolate bar.

**Yields:** 1 serving
**Total Time:** 10 minutes

## Ingredients

* 1 (1.55 ounce / 44g) bar milk chocolate, chopped
* 2/3 cup milk (or more to taste) (about 160 ml)
* 1 pinch ground cinnamon

## Instructions

1. Place the chopped chocolate pieces in a saucepan over medium-low heat. 
2. Add the milk and whisk constantly until the chocolate is completely melted and well blended (about 5 minutes). 
3. Whisk in the pinch of cinnamon. 
4. Remove from heat. Add more milk if desired. 
5. Serve immediately in a mug and enjoy!
